Washer Repair

Call to Schedule

A broken washer means dirty laundry piling up fast. We diagnose and repair washing machines of all types — top-load, front-load, high-efficiency, compact — across every major brand including Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, GE, Maytag, and Kenmore.

Our technicians carry common parts on the truck so most washer repairs are completed in a single visit. We'll tell you exactly what's wrong and what it costs before we touch anything.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Won't start or turn on
  • Not spinning or agitating
  • Won't drain properly
  • Leaking water on the floor
  • Shaking or vibrating excessively
  • Makes loud grinding or banging noise
  • Lid or door won't lock
  • Fills with water then stops
  • Error codes on display
  • Smells musty or burnt

Dryer Repair

Call to Schedule

A dryer that won't heat or takes three cycles to dry a load isn't just inconvenient — it's running up your utility bill. We repair both gas and electric dryers from all major manufacturers.

Whether it's a failed heating element, a bad thermal fuse, a worn drum belt, or a control board issue, we pinpoint the problem quickly and fix it right the first time.

Common Issues We Fix

  • No heat or not enough heat
  • Takes too long to dry clothes
  • Drum won't turn or tumble
  • Won't start at all
  • Makes squealing or thumping noise
  • Shuts off before clothes are dry
  • Runs but produces no heat
  • Door won't close or latch
  • Gas dryer won't ignite
  • Overheating or burning smell

Refrigerator Repair

Call to Schedule

When your refrigerator fails, the clock is ticking. We treat refrigerator calls as high priority and offer same-day appointments when available to protect your food and minimize loss.

We service all styles — French door, side-by-side, top-freezer, bottom-freezer, and built-in units. All major brands including Samsung, LG, GE, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, Sub-Zero, and Frigidaire.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Not cooling or not cold enough
  • Freezer works but fridge section doesn't
  • Ice maker not making ice
  • Water dispenser not working
  • Leaking water inside or under unit
  • Frost buildup in freezer
  • Running constantly or cycling too fast
  • Loud or unusual noises
  • Fridge is too warm, freezer too cold
  • Control panel or display issues

Garbage Disposal Repair

Call to Schedule

A jammed or dead garbage disposal disrupts your whole kitchen routine. We repair and replace all brands of garbage disposals — InSinkErator, Waste King, Moen, and others.

Most disposal repairs are quick fixes, but if your unit is beyond repair, we'll recommend and install a quality replacement at a fair price.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Disposal is jammed and won't spin
  • Humming but not grinding
  • Won't turn on at all
  • Leaking from bottom or side
  • Makes loud rattling or grinding noise
  • Slow to drain or clogged
  • Reset button won't stay in
  • Burning smell from motor

Oven Repair

Call to Schedule

We repair both gas and electric ovens, including wall ovens and range-style units. Whether your oven won't heat, heats unevenly, or the electronic controls have gone haywire, we've got you covered.

We service all major brands including GE, Whirlpool, KitchenAid, Samsung, LG, Bosch, Wolf, and Frigidaire.

Common Issues We Fix

  • Oven won't heat up
  • Uneven baking or hot spots
  • Temperature runs too hot or cold
  • Gas oven won't ignite
  • Broiler not working
  • Oven door won't close properly
  • Self-clean feature not working
  • Control board or display issues
  • Oven light not working

Stove / Range / Cooktop

Call to Schedule

Whether you have a gas range, electric range, or induction cooktop, we repair all types and configurations. Burner issues, igniter problems, control failures — we handle it all.

We work on freestanding ranges, slide-in ranges, drop-in cooktops, and built-in units from all major manufacturers.

  • Constant clicking sound
