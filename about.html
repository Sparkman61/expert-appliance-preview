Our Story

Serving Charlotte
the Right Way

Expert Appliance Service was founded to give Charlotte homeowners something that's gotten hard to find: a repair company you can actually trust. No runaround, no inflated quotes, no parts ordered just to pad a bill.

We're certified technicians who've spent years in the field diagnosing and repairing appliances of every make and model. We know how manufacturers build these machines, which means we find the real problem — not just the symptom — and fix it the first time.
